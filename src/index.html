Phaedra

Phaedra is my1 body of essays on anything; ranging from materials engineering, political though, book reviews, philosophy or poetry. All of these memos (or documents) will share the same format in a RFO (Request-For-Opinion) as a rip off of IETF RFC2. I am doing this to challenge myself to think deeper, write coherently, about on concept, and challenge the reader to save themselves from the attention collapse. This is Phaedra.

As is assumed in the O in RFO, I request for any opinions you may have on my work. I do not believe in comments, but in thorough critique, which you can reach to me by email.
